Whole Microbiome Analysis Pricing

Our full service pricing offers the best value in the market for comprehensive, sensitive, and quantitative microbial community analysis. We offer discounts for large orders and standing orders. We accept extracted DNA from any sample as well as frozen water filters for a small surcharge. The initial minimum order quantity is 15 samples to ensure that you receive high quality results.
