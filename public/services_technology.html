Third-Generation, Award-winning Technology

Proprietary PhyloChip™ technology, licensed exclusively from Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, (LBNL) was invented in the laboratory of Dr. Gary Andersen.

The PhyloChip received the Wall Street Journal’s top 2008 Technology Innovation Award in the Environment category and was highlighted as one of the 21 top breakthroughs at LBNL in the past 80 years, “that improved the world…and our lives.”

The PhyloChip assay is a third-generation technology combining a custom-designed, manufactured microarray utilizing proprietary probe selection and bioinformatics to determine the presence and relative abundance of more than 50,000 bacterial and archaeal taxa from microbial DNA samples extracted from any source. Our intellectual property (IP) covers a broad range of systems, computer logic, and methods claims.
